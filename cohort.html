Key Insight

Green cohort users (those who practice 3+ days during trial) convert to monthly at 66.7% vs 50.4% for non-green — a 16.3pp gap. Green share peaked at 37.7% (Jan 5) but has since declined to ~30%. Each 1pp increase in green share lifts blended CVR by ~0.16pp. Prioritizing engagement during the trial window is the highest-leverage retention lever.
